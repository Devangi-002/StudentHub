About StudentHub

StudentHub Portal is a web-based platform designed to help students manage their academic information in one place. Students can view courses, attendance, results, notifications, and profile details through a simple and user-friendly interface.

Our Mission

  • Provide easy access to academic information.
  • Improve communication between students and faculty.
  • Make academic management simple and efficient.

Features

  • Student Dashboard
  • Course Management
  • Attendance Tracking
  • Result Management
  • Notifications
  • Student Profile
  • Contact Support

Technologies Used

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
